The perfect platform for delivering effective digital education in prisons.

Offline Education

Offline education puts the student in control of their learning. With access to education in cell, students learn at their own pace and utilise their time constructively.

Best Choice For Your Needs

You can choose the best device to fit your needs. Core Prison Education works on tablets, laptops, and PCs.

Courses

Courses offer students an enriched learning experience, with content delivered in multiple media types, including text, images, and video.

Coursework

Whether it’s in the classroom or in cell, students can read documentation, write assignments, edit spreadsheets, and submit coursework in a few simple steps.

Tests

Tests are a great motivator and essential for tracking progress. They’re also a crucial mechanism for identifying students who need additional help.

Communications
