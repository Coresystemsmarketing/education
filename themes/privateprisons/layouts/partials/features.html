Whatever The Prison, We've Got You Covered

The perfect platform for delivering effective prisoners services in prisons.

Reception Prisons

Reception prisons will manage remand prisoners and those with a short time to serve. Core's platform will support prisoners through this stressful period by providing the following tools:

    ☑ Family Communications (messaging)
    ☑ Supports Listener Communications
    ☑ Induction Information
    ☑ Requests Module

Training Prisons

Training Prisons will mainly house longer-term prisoners. Core's platform empowers prisoners to change their outlook, by building resilience, confidence and hope for the future during this extended period. The following tools facilitate this change, and prepare prisoners for resettlement.

    ☑ OMiC Planning Tool
    ☑ Online/Offline Education Platform
    ☑ Family Communications (messaging)
    ☑ Life Skills Training

Resettlement Prisons

Resettlement prisons prepare people for release into the community. Leaving prison can invoke mixed emotions amongst prisoners. Core's platform eases this transition; transfering responsbility to the prisoner, facilitating community engagement, and providing through-the-gate solutions:

    ☑ ROTL app
    ☑ Calendar/Planner app
    ☑ Jobs app
    ☑ Accommodation app
